WHY TRULY?

I started Truly Liz Photography while still in high school. After nearly four years of dabbling in the art and falling in love I decided that this is what I want to do with the rest  of my life, capturing smiles and memories. When trying to name my company, I wrote down the things I value most. At the top was truth and honest, so I turned that into my brand. My promise when you hire me is that you are hiring a person, not a camera. Not only do I want to capture your smiles, but I want to play a part in making them! I do my best to be a friend during your session. I'll even do my best to memorize everyone's names, because that's how much I care about your pictures being authentic to you!

ABOUT LIZ

Hey Y’all! So happy you are here! Before we get to you, let me tell you a little about me! I am an 18 year old self proclaimed girl boss (though my family and friends like to call me a perfectionist). I am a lover of diet coke, football and working with kids! I’ve been an auntie since I was six years old and it is my greatest passion in life, photography being the second!
